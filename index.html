Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

最后更新：2026年7月

概述

专注的我（Focus）是一款个人效率工具。我们非常重视您的隐私。本政策说明我们如何处理您的数据。

我们收集哪些数据

本应用本身不收集任何个人信息。所有数据（包括打卡记录、专注时长、任务列表）均由您本人创建，仅存储在：

  • 您的设备本地存储中
  • 您个人的 iCloud 账户中（用于多设备同步）

开发者无法访问您 iCloud 中的任何数据。

iCloud 同步

如果您启用了 iCloud，应用会使用 Apple 的 CloudKit 服务将数据同步到您的其他设备。此数据受您的 Apple ID 和 iCloud 账户保护，遵循 Apple 隐私政策。

通知

应用可能会请求发送通知权限，用于在番茄钟结束时提醒您。通知内容不包含任何个人数据，且不会离开您的设备。

第三方分享

我们不会将您的任何数据出售、出租或以任何方式分享给第三方。

数据删除

您可以随时在应用内使用"重置数据"功能删除所有本地数据。如需删除 iCloud 数据，可在 iPhone 设置 → Apple ID → iCloud → 管理账户存储 中操作。

联系我们

如有任何隐私相关问题，请通过 App Store 页面联系开发者。

Overview

Focus (专注的我) is a personal productivity app. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle your data.

What Data We Collect

This app does not collect any personal information. All data (including check-in records, focus durations, and task lists) is created by you and stored only in:

  • Your device's local storage
  • Your personal iCloud account (for cross-device sync)

The developer has no access to any data in your iCloud account.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, the app uses Apple's CloudKit service to sync your data to your other devices. This data is protected by your Apple ID and iCloud account and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send notifications to alert you when a Pomodoro session ends. Notification content contains no personal data and never leaves your device.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties in any form.

Data Deletion

You can delete all local data at any time using the "Reset Data" function within the app. To delete iCloud data, go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account Storage.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact the developer through the App Store page.
